About Me

I'm Abaleke Promise, a passionate Full LAMP Stack Developer and Creative Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. With over 2 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in building elegant, responsive websites and intuitive user interfaces that bridge the gap between functionality and design.

I studied and worked at Infotech, where I deepened my expertise in technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript,React, MySQL and PHP , alongside tools like PixelLab, Adobe Photoshop, and GitHub. Whether it's crafting a clean UI, optimizing performance, or bringing a design to life with animation, I’m all about delivering high-impact results.

Outside of coding, you’ll find me designing logos, experimenting with layouts in Illustrator, or exploring new ways to improve user experience. I love collaborating with forward-thinking teams to solve real-world problems through beautiful digital solutions.
